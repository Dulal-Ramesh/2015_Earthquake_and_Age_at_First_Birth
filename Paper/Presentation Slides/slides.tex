\section{Introduction}
%------------------------------------------------
\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{Background}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Nepal was hit by a 7.8 magnitude earthquake on $25^{th}$ of April and $12^{th}$ of May in the year 2015.
        \item 14/75 districts: worst affected and 31/75: affected
        \item 500,000 houses were destroyed totally and 250,000 were partially damaged.
        \item About 9,000 deaths and 22,300 casualties
        \item 55\% of the casualties resulted into women and child mortality
        \item 446 public health facilities including 5 hospitals were completely destroyed
        \item 765 health facilities were partially damaged
        
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

%------------------------------------------------
\begin{frame}
    \frametitle{Contd...}
    \begin{itemize}
    \item Government started providing emergency relied as tarps, food, Household goods, corrugated galvanized sheets or even cash.
        \item Government provided decided to provide the cash assistance on June 1, 2015
        \item The cash assistance was 30,000 for funeral service, 15,000 for severely hit houses and 3,000 for partially damaged houses. 
        \item The government initiated Local Government and Community Program Grant with cash assistance to be provided through local government
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

%------------------------------------------------
\begin{frame}
	\frametitle{Question}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item What were the effects of 2015 Earthquake in Nepal on infant mortality? What were the factors responsible for the infant mortality?  
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
